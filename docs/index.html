Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Parked Car Finder respects your privacy. This application is designed to work entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Information Collection and Use

Parked Car Finder does not collect, share, or sell any personal or sensitive user data. All information used by the app remains locally on your device.

Permissions Used

The app requests the following permissions strictly to provide its core functionality:

Location (Precise and Approximate)

Location access is used solely to save your parked car’s location on your own device so you can navigate back to it later. This location data is stored locally on your phone and is never transmitted, shared, or uploaded anywhere.

Foreground Service

A foreground service is used to keep a parking timer running while the app is not in the foreground. This allows the app to accurately track parking duration and calculate parking cost without interruption.

Notifications

Notification permission allows the app to display an ongoing notification while parking is active. This notification shows your parking status and estimated cost so you can check it at a glance without opening the app.

Data Storage

All data related to your parking session (location, start time, and rate) is stored locally on your device. The app does not use cloud storage, analytics services, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.

Data Sharing

Parked Car Finder does not share any data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via the app store listing.
